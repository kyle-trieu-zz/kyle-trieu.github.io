A Little Bit About Me

I'm a senior at UC Berkeley studying Mechanical Engineering, with a Certificate in the Human-Centered Design Course Thread, and pursuing the Berkeley Certificate in Design Innovation. 

I am passionate about product development and engineering design; I want to create products that will make a difference in people's lives.

I'm also super excited to be joining Oculus as a Product Design Engineer in August.

Some of my interests include product design engineering, the human-centered design process, industrial design, and design ethics, encompassing the impacts & implications of our design choices.

You can also find me competing in urban dance competitions, modeling and making 3D printed gadgets, listening to 99 Percent Invisible podcasts, participating in Render Weekly, or petting every dog I see.
